Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving business ecosystem, Muaki Business Park represents the vision of sustainable growth and community development.

Excellence Through Diversity

Muaki Business Park stands as a testament to the power of diversified business excellence. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a business ecosystem where different industries could thrive together, sharing resources, knowledge, and opportunities.

Today, we operate across six distinct sectors - real estate, construction, automotive, healthcare technology, fitness, and investments - each contributing to our collective strength and community impact.

Welcome to Muaki Business Park - where engineering precision meets entrepreneurial vision, and sustainable development becomes reality. As an electrical and civil engineer turned business leader, I founded this company with a fundamental understanding: strong foundations create lasting success, whether in construction or commerce.

My background in engineering and accounting taught me the importance of structural integrity, careful planning, and building for the long term. These principles now guide our diversified business ecosystem spanning multiple industries. From designing infrastructure to developing properties, from automotive services to healthcare technology - each venture is built on solid engineering principles and strategic business thinking.

What sets us apart is our systematic approach to business development, rooted in engineering methodology. We analyze, design, and build sustainable solutions. Our construction division leverages my engineering expertise, while our other companies benefit from the same methodical approach to problem-solving and quality assurance.

The communities of Limpopo and Gauteng are not just our markets - they're our foundation. Every infrastructure project we complete, every job we create, and every service we provide is designed to deliver maximum community impact. We're building more than businesses; we're constructing the framework for regional economic growth.

Looking ahead, our expansion plans are as carefully engineered as our buildings. We're not just growing businesses; we're architecting a legacy of sustainable development, community empowerment, and economic transformation that will stand the test of time.

Thank you for trusting in our vision. Together, we're not just building businesses - we're engineering the future of our communities.

Nhlamulo Chuma

Chief Executive Officer
