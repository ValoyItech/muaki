About Muaki Group

With over 15 years of experience, Muaki Group is a diversified South African company specializing in civil engineering, electrical services including full backup power solutions, domestic & commercial construction, air conditioning installations, and more.

In addition to construction and engineering, we proudly operate several hardware stores and manage a portfolio of rental apartments across South Africa. Our multidisciplinary team delivers reliable, high-quality services with a focus on safety, innovation, and long-term partnerships.
